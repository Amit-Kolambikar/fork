During my first year at Google, I was an Associate Product Manager at Google, working on Firebase developer experience. I was the product owner of the the Firebase Console, the redeisgned website, developer docs, and the Android Studio plugin.

My work involved working with many Firebase contributing teams on cross-product features in the Firebase console, aligned multiple stakeholders for a complete redesign of our website, as well as working with the Android Studio team to build the future of the Firebase Assistant.
