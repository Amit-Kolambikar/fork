Hello, and welcome to my website! My name is Dan Schlosser, and I am a jazz composer and Computer Science student at Columbia University. About

I am a computer scientist, a jazz composer, and a student. I am a technology enthusiast, a vibraphonist, and an aspiring designer. In my free time, I enjoy working on personal projects, discovering new music, binging on television, and plunking away on my 10-year-old electric piano. My favorite song right now is Saguaro by Ron Carter.
