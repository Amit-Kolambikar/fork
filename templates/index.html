compyser

Because who in the music industry has time for inspiration?

Developed at the Spring 2013 HACKNY student hackathon, compyser is a composition tool that writes and plays back a song with just a few notes of input from a MIDI keyboard. Compyser intelligently assigns the input rhythm and harmony, based on tweakable values and it's understanding of basic chord function. As a bonus, the name of the compyser window offers a suggestion for song title, straight from Tumblr!

  • Python
  • WX Python
  • Music Theory
