When I moved to San Francisco from the east coast, I started up a podcast with Nathan Savoy, one of my oldest friends, about the video games that we played as kids. We chat and joke around about all kinds of video games (both well-known and lesser-known ones) that we played years ago and still can't stop thinking about.

Listen to our latest episode!

Some of my favorite episodes so far include Episode 2: Batman Arkham Asylum where I give away the true extend of my Batman nerdom, Episode 4: SSX Tricky, where we discuss the best snowboarding video game of all time, and Episode 5: The Simpsons Hit and Run, where we express our surprise that a TV-adaptation video game could actually be good. Give us a listen!
