Eventum is an open source content management system for event-driven websites, built in Python and Flask. I wrote Eventum from scratch for ADI, the tech club at Columbia, when I was redesigning their website in the summer of 2014. Building Eventum was a massive undertaking, including almost a complete rewrite a few years later. Checkout the blog post I wrote motivating Eventum, or contribute on GitHub.
